## 👨‍💻 About Me

Most enterprise backends don't fail loudly. They slow down, silently — until your biggest client notices first.

Bloated queries. Monoliths pretending to be microservices. APIs that hold at 100 users and crumble at 10,000. I've seen it across **GST platforms**, **vendor verification systems**, **asset tracking tools**, and **enterprise workflow applications** — and I've been the one called in to fix it.

I'm a Backend Developer with **4 years of experience** specializing in **.NET Core, distributed systems, and high-performance APIs** — building systems designed to scale before they need to, not after they break.

What I care about:
- Writing code that the next engineer actually wants to maintain
- Systems that degrade gracefully, not catastrophically
- APIs designed for consumers, not just for tests to pass

Currently at **Microvista Technologies** · Previously **Casepoint**
